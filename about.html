    The Empire Recruitment Agency

    We work professionally and ethically to give our clients the most effective solutions which help them achieve their dreams & goals.

    According to the resolution № 725 of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan on September 12, 2018, On further improvement and radical revision of the unified employment system of the citizens of the Republic of Uzbekistan abroad, “The Empire” Private Employment Agency was established and registered as of November 6, 2018.

    According to the Law of the Republic of Uzbekistan № 501 dated on 16 October 2018 On Private Employment Agencies of the Republic of Uzbekistan, the Ministry of Labor of the Republic of Uzbekistan certified “The Empire” Private Employment Agency to be eligible to carry out employment activities abroad and provided License under № 0019.
